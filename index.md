# Welcome to Inquiro Media Labs

Inquiro Media Labs is a digital garden documenting the development of an AI-powered application built with large language models such as ChatGPT, DeepSeek, Gemini, and Claude. It’s a space for curious minds to explore how generative AI, custom business logic, thoughtful interfaces, and workflow automation come together to solve real-world problems.

The name Inquiro comes from Latin, meaning “to inquire” or “to investigate.”
